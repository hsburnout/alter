GETTING STARTED

Step 1:

Read over read_me.txt with your child and make sure they understand.

This file can be found on the desktop.

Step 2:

Help your child setup a wifi connection.

This is used to access tutorials, submit their creations, talk to chatgpt, and install standard Ubuntu security updates.

Step 3:

Go over the submission rules with your child.

These can be found by clicking the submission shortcut on the desktop.

Step 4:

Create chatgpt account and login.
